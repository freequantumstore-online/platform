Quantum Circuit Builder

Apply Hadamard (H) gates to put qubits in superposition, then measure. Click a gate slot to toggle H. All qubits are measured to produce random bits.

↓ Random bits feed into BB84 key exchange
2
BB84 Key Distribution

Alice uses the random bits from Step 1. She picks random bases to encode them. Bob picks random bases to measure. Where bases match, bits are kept as the shared key.

↓ Shared key feeds into encryption
3
XOR Cipher Encryption

Use the quantum-derived shared key to encrypt and decrypt a message with XOR cipher.
